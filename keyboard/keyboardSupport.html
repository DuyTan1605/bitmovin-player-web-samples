HTML5 Adaptive Streaming Player for MPEG-DASH & HLS

Keyboard Control demo for the Bitmovin Player, use your keyboard to control video playback.

  • space
    Play/Pause
  • m
    Mute/Unmute
  • f
    Enter fullscreen
  • esc
    Exit fullscreen
  • right
    Seek +5 Seconds
  • ctrl+right / command+right
    Seek +10 Seconds
  • left
    Seek -5 Seconds
  • ctrl+left / command+left
    Seek -10 seconds
  • up
    Volume +5
  • ctrl+up / command+up
    Volume +10
  • down
    Volume -5
  • ctrl+down / command+down
    Volume -10
